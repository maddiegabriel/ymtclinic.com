Michèle McBeigh, RMT

Michèle has lived in the Burlington/Oakville area since 1991. She is the mother of two boys and is fluent in both French and English. She began her working life as a purchasing manager in Québec. A career in massage therapy has been a life-long dream which Michèle pursued once her sons were older.

Michèle is dedicated to helping you improve your health and sense of wellbeing by offering careful assessment, timely treatment and providing you with beneficial home remedial exercises. Michèle is a life long learner and is continually updating her knowledge and education in the field of massage therapy.

Experience

Michèle graduated from the Canadian Therapeutic College in 2009 with a diploma in Massage Therapy and Hydrotherapy. Since then, she has gained experience in treating a wide variety of conditions. Michèle has worked with physiotherapists as well as in long term care facilities and retirement homes.
Presently, Michele operates a home-based clinic working with clients of all ages including athletes, individuals living with chronic pain, pre and post partum women and the elderly.

Benefits of Massage Therapy

Massage therapy involves manipulation of the muscles and other soft tissues in order to relieve muscle tension, increase flexibility, promote relaxation and decrease pain. Massage therapy can be an integral part of your overall injury rehabilitation program or can also be an excellent form of preventative care for promoting stress relief and reducing the risk of muscle strains that can result from excessive muscle tone.

Benefits of Massage Therapy

Alleviate muscle soreness and pain
